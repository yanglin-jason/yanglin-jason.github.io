About me

I’m currently a researcher in Noah’s Ark Lab, Huawei. My research is to empower the next-generation networks with the artificial intelligence. I’m particularly interested in: bayesian optimization, graph learning, and reinforcement learning.

I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I worked with Prof. Qian Zhang in Huawei-HKUST Innovation Lab there. Before that, I obtained my B.S degree in Computer Science from South China University of Technology (SCUT ) in June 2008.
