About me

I’m currently a senior researcher at Noah’s Ark Lab, Huawei. My research mainly focuses on improving the complex reasoning capability of LLMs, with particular interests in: efficient post-training of foundation models, data synthesis, and advanced reasoning techniques for LLMs. I also have extensive experience in Bayesian optimization and reinforcement learning.

I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), where I worked with Prof. Qian Zhang in the Huawei-HKUST Innovation Lab there. Before that, I obtained my B.S degree in Computer Science from South China University of Technology (SCUT ) in June 2008.
